Dog Sitting

IN YOUR OWN HOME


We understand some dog owners would much prefer their dog be able to stay in their own environment, where they feel safe and able to thrive. We are happy to provide a dog sitting service in your own home where one of our staff will stay at your house for the duration of your time away. We will meet you for a pre-assessment, where you will have the time and space to communicate all the necessary information about your dog's routine, likes, dislikes and also share any important details of staying in your home. We will provide daily updates by text, sending photos and any other information you'd like to know. We want you to feel at ease during your time away, and know that our highly trained and dedicated staff will take the best care of your beloved furry friend.


PRICE PER NIGHT: €35 (10% discount for a week or longer)

IN ONE OF OUR STAFF'S HOMES


We also have dedicated staff who have made their homes available to host your dogs. We will meet you for a pre-assessment where you can talk to the staff member, and if you want to we can facilitate this at their home wherever possible to make sure you feel comfortable having your dog stay away from their normal surroundings. You will also have a chance to tell us anything we may need to know about your dog, their routine, likes and dislikes. We will let you know if the staff member has their own dog, and make sure this won't be any issue. We find that the dogs do have an enriching time playing together, but also know for some dogs this isn't suitable, so we will try to find the best option for you. Daily updates and photos will be sent by text.


PRICE PER NIGHT: €38 (10% discount for a week or longer)
