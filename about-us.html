Who Are We?

Pawfection was founded in 2015 in Galway, Ireland. We noticed a growing number of people were not happy leaving their dog in kennels while they went away. We decided to open our doors as an alternative solution to dog care for individuals and families who needed a dog sitter. Showing the dogs in our care all the needed 'affection' while they are away from their owners is our mantra.


We are a small team of dedicated staff, passionate about the care and well being of all the dogs we have the pleasure of sitting.

We believe that each dog is individual, with a unique personality. Some dogs thrive on new adventures, while others need the stability of their own environment, and routines to keep them happy and healthy.


We endeavour to facilitate a stress free stay with your dog, whether in your own home or one of ours. our aim is to give your dog the best experience, while you relax and enjoy your time away.
