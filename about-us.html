Who Are We?

Pawfection was founded in 2015 in Galway, Ireland. we had noticed a growing number of people were not happy leaving their dog in a kennel while they went away. we decided to open our doors for an alternative solution to dog care for individuals and families who needed a dog sitter. showing the dogs in our care all the needed 'affection' while they are separated from their owners.


We are a small team of dedicated staff, passionate about the care and well being of all the dogs we have the pleasure of sitting.
